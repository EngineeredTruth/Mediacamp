Courses

Jumpcut Academy

Jumpcut Academy is the premiere online course for YouTubers. Their own YouTube channel, Simple Pickup, has over 2.7 million subscribers. They also get other prominient YouTubers, such as JK News, Vitaly, Sexy Simple Stupid, to teach classes as well. A lot of value at an affordable subscription of $17/month.

Creator Academy

The creator academy is the official course from YouTube. They bring on top youtubers from a spectrum of genres to give advice on how grow a channel. Most of the advice is cliche, but it is the best free resource for learning how to be a YouTuber.
